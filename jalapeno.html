Jalapeno Popper Cups

Didn't have enough Jalapenos to make full out poppers, but improvised for a creamy and spicy appetizer. You'll want
to eat them right away, but they will be molten hot so be careful!

Ingredients

  • 12 mini phyllo tart shells

  • 4 ounces cream cheese, softened

  • Â½ cup shredded Cheddar cheese

  • 2 jalapeno peppers, seeded and chopped

  • 1 tablespoon hot pepper sauce

  • bacon bits

Directions

Step 1
Preheat an oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C). Place phyllo cups onto a baking sheet.

Step 2
Stir together cream cheese, Cheddar cheese, jalapenos, and hot sauce in a bowl.
Spoon mixture into phyllo cups. Sprinkle bacon bits on top. Bake in preheated
oven until golden brown, about 15 to 20 minutes. Serve warm.
